Neural-Logix: Semiconductor Supply Hub

An AI-augmented live tracking and predictive operations dashboard designed for Nvidia, Broadcom, and Intel. Monitoring trans-Pacific silicon routes from Asia manufacturing hubs to US assembly locations.

Trans-Pacific Node Architecture

The semiconductor lifecycle is highly distributed. Raw wafers and fabricated dies originate primarily in East Asian foundries (e.g., TSMC, Samsung). These are transported via secured, environmentally-controlled freight to stateside facilities for final assembly, packaging, and integration by leading US tech giants.
